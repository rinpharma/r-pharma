R/Pharma Conference

The conference is a relatively small, scientifically & industry oriented, collegial event focused on the use of R in the development of pharmaceuticals.

The 2022 Conference will run Nov. 8-10th, with workshops the week before. The call for abstracts is now open, and will close June 20th.
